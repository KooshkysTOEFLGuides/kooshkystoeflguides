Relative Clauses

Relative clauses let you identify, describe, and add information about nouns inside a larger sentence. Mastering them makes your writing more precise, compact, and sophisticated.

Why this matters

Separate sentences: I spoke to the professor. She teaches syntax.

With a relative clause: I spoke to the professor who teaches syntax.
