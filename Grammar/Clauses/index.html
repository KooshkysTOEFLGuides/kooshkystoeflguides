Phrases and Clauses

This is a must-know subject if you want to learn TOEFL-level grammar. Take the time to work through these notes carefully and in order.

Why this matters

Punctuation rule: A semicolon normally joins two closely related independent clauses.

If you do not know what an independent clause is, even a simple punctuation rule like this becomes much harder to understand and use correctly.
