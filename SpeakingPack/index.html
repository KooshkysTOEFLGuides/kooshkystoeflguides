Kooshky's Speaking Collection

Speaking Packs

These packs help you build the vocabulary, ideas, and examples you need to speak clearly and confidently about common TOEFL topics.

How to use the packs

Choose a topic, study the useful language and idea bank, and then practise producing complete spoken answers without memorising a fixed script.

Topic collection

Choose a speaking topic

The packs are independent, so you can study them in any order.
