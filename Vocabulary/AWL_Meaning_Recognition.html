Academic Word List: Meaning Recognition

A complete Anki deck for learning the Academic Word List—the vocabulary foundation I recommend most strongly for TOEFL students.

Why this deck?

The Academic Word List contains about 600 important word families used across academic subjects. Knowing them will not cover every word you need for the TOEFL, but not knowing them makes the test much harder than it needs to be.

TOEFL Resources makes a similar recommendation: I think it is probably better to just study the Academic Word List. — TOEFL Resources

I built this deck to make that list easier to study properly rather than memorizing one vague translation for each word.

  • Relevant meanings and parts of speech
  • Audio pronunciation
  • Word-family forms
  • Plenty of contextual examples
